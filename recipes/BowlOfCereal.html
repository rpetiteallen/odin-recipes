How to Make a Refreshing Bowl of Cereal

Today you will learn how to make a bowl of cereal using some of your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Cereal
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pour the desired amount of cereal into the bowl
  2. Pour milk into the cereal over the bowl until it reaches your preferred level
  3. Optionally stir and enjoy immediately
