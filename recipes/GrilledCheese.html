Grilled Cheese Sandwich

How to Make a Tasty Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Today you will learn how to make a tasty Grilled Cheese Sandwich using some of your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 - 3 Slices of Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • Preheated Skillet on medium-low heat

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place one slice of bread, buttered-side down into the skillet and top with cheese
  3. Place second slice of bread, buttered-side up on top of cheese topped slice in the pan
  4. Cover the skillet with a lid and allow 2-4 minutes for the cheese to melt while the bottom toasts
  5. After 2-4 minutes remove the lid and flip the sandwich with a spatula once the bottom is golden brown
  6. Remove from the pan and let cool for 1-2 minutes
  7. Serve and Enjoy
