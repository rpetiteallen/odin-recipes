Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

How to Make a Tasty Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Today you will learn how to make a tasty Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich using some of your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread the Jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Gently press the peanut butter and jelly slices together
  4. Enjoy
